About This Tool

The synthetic voice is a result of Part 4 Project done by Fuki Babasaki and Jenny Wang, The University of Auckland, 2022.

The project was supervised by James, Sally Akevai Nicholas, Catherine Watson and Justine Hui.

We thank the speakers of the Cook Island Māori speech database for agreeing to share their voice for synthetic voice development
