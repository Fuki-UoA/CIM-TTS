About This Tool

This synthetic voice was built for a Part 4 Project
Built by Fuki Babasaki and Jenny Wang, from The University of Auckland, 2022.

The project was supervised by Jesin James, Sally Akevai Nicholas, Catherine Watson and Justine Hui.

We thank the speakers of the Cook Island Māori speech database for agreeing to share their voice for language revitilisation efforts, and synthesised speech development.
